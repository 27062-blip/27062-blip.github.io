Required points

Law overview

Title of the law

Citizens' AI Rights Act.

What AI problem it solves

Hidden AI decisions that can unfairly affect jobs, school, credit, and services.

Main rules of the law

Notice when AI is used, right to human review, and safety checks for high-risk systems.

Who benefits

Workers, students, families, consumers, and responsible builders.

Possible criticisms

Some may say it slows innovation, is too broad, or needs stronger enforcement.

Why voters should support it

It protects people with transparency, accountability, and real rights in AI decisions.

What this law does

Simple rights for people

01

Know

People must be told when AI makes a major decision.

02

Challenge

People can request a human review.

03

Safety

High-risk AI must pass checks before use.

Why this matters

People first, systems second.

No person should lose work, school, or services because of a hidden algorithm.

This law gives transparency, review, and accountability in one clear framework.
